A QA Team That Ships With Your Sprints

Two dedicated QA engineers with AI-powered test generation, embedded in your Slack and CI/CD — your starter tier for continuous quality.

Duration: Ongoing Team: 2 QA Engineers + Test Lead oversight
The Challenge

You might be experiencing...

We need QA but can't justify hiring a full-time team yet — we just need two solid engineers who can keep up with our sprint velocity.
Our developers are writing their own tests and it's slowing down feature delivery. We need dedicated QA people who own the test layer.
We tried freelance QA testers from Upwork and the ramp-up time killed us — by the time they understood our product, the sprint was over.
We're shipping weekly and our manual QA process is the bottleneck. We need people who can automate as they test.

The QA Sprint Team is your fastest path to dedicated QA engineers — without the overhead of hiring, onboarding, and managing an internal team.

How It Works

You get two remote QA engineers who join your Slack, attend your standups, and execute testing within your sprint cadence. A test lead oversees their output, reviews test quality, and ensures coverage aligns with your product priorities.

AI-powered test generation accelerates their work — your team uses AI to draft test cases from user stories and acceptance criteria, then refines and executes them with human judgment. This means your two-person team delivers the output of a traditional three-person team.

Who This Is For

The QA Sprint Team is built for startups at the stage where quality matters but a full QA department does not yet make sense:

  • Seed to Series A companies shipping weekly and tired of developers context-switching to write tests
  • Product teams that need QA as a service without a 6-month hiring process
  • Engineering leads who want a dedicated QA function they can scale up or down with their roadmap

What You Get

Every sprint, your QA team delivers:

  1. Test cases — AI-generated and human-reviewed, covering the sprint’s feature scope
  2. Bug reports — with reproduction steps, severity classification, and device/browser context
  3. Regression testing — automated checks that ensure new features do not break existing functionality
  4. Monthly insights — coverage trends, bug patterns, and recommendations that compound over time

Your team is not a black box. You see every test case, every bug report, and every coverage metric in real time through your existing tools.

Our Approach

Engagement Phases

Week 1

Onboarding & Integration

Your QA Sprint Team is assigned, onboarded to your product, and integrated into your Slack channels and CI/CD pipeline. We review your existing test assets, set up AI test generation tooling, and establish sprint QA workflows.

Week 2+

Sprint Embedding

Your team participates in sprint planning, writes and executes test cases using AI-assisted generation, runs exploratory testing, and reports bugs with full reproduction steps. Test results are visible in your CI/CD dashboard.

Monthly

Continuous Improvement

Monthly QA health reports covering test coverage trends, bug escape rates, automation progress, and recommendations for the next sprint cycle. Your test lead reviews all output and adjusts the QA strategy based on product changes.

How quickly can the team start?

Your QA Sprint Team is assigned within 48 hours of contract signing. Week 1 is dedicated onboarding — by Week 2 your team is executing test cases in your sprint. We maintain a bench of pre-vetted QA engineers so there is no recruitment delay.

What time zones do your QA engineers work in?

We match your team's working hours. Our engineers work across UTC+0 to UTC+8, with the majority covering European and Asian business hours. For US-based teams, we offer overlap windows and async handoff protocols that ensure continuous coverage.

Can we scale up to a larger team later?

Yes. The QA Sprint Team is designed as your starting tier. When your product or team grows, you can upgrade to our Managed QA service (4 engineers + automation lead) or QA Center of Excellence (full program) without re-onboarding — your test assets, documentation, and team relationships carry forward.
