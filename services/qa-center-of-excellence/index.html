The QA Center of Excellence is remote.qa’s enterprise-grade QA program - for companies where quality is not a feature but a business requirement.

What a QA Center of Excellence Delivers

This is not a team bolted onto your engineering org. It is a managed QA program with its own leadership, strategy, and accountability:

QA Director - owns your quality strategy across all products. Reports to your CTO or VP Engineering with quarterly governance reviews, quality trend analysis, and strategic recommendations.

QA Lead + Automation Architect - manages day-to-day operations, owns the test automation architecture, and orchestrates AI-powered test generation at scale across your entire product portfolio.

6-10 QA Engineers - dedicated engineers matched to your products, tech stack, and time zones. Structured as sub-teams per product with shared standards and tooling.

Follow-the-Sun Coverage

For companies operating across time zones, the QA CoE provides follow-the-sun testing - your products are being tested around the clock with documented handoff protocols between shifts. No gaps, no overnight blind spots.

AI Orchestration at Scale

At the CoE level, AI is not just assisting individual engineers - it is orchestrating your entire test automation strategy:

  • Automated test generation from user stories, PRs, and product specs across all products
  • Cross-product regression analysis - AI identifies when a change in one product could affect another
  • Self-healing test infrastructure - AI maintains selectors, updates assertions, and flags structural changes before they cause flaky tests
  • Coverage gap detection - continuous analysis of untested code paths prioritised by business impact

SLA-Backed Quality

Every QA Center of Excellence engagement includes contractual SLAs - bug escape rate targets, response time commitments, and coverage guarantees backed by audit trails. This is the documentation your enterprise customers, investors, and compliance teams require.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the QA Center of Excellence different from Managed QA?

Managed QA gives you a dedicated squad for a single product. The QA Center of Excellence is a program-level engagement - it covers multiple products, includes a QA Director who owns your quality strategy, provides follow-the-sun coverage, and delivers SLA-backed commitments with audit trails. It is designed for companies where quality is a board-level concern.

How much does a QA Center of Excellence cost?

Book a free discovery call to discuss your project scope and get a custom quote.

Can we start with Managed QA and upgrade?

Yes - this is the recommended path for most companies. Start with Managed QA for your primary product, validate the working relationship and ROI, then expand to a QA Center of Excellence as you add products or teams. All test assets, team relationships, and institutional knowledge carry forward.

Do you provide compliance documentation for audits?

Yes. The QA Center of Excellence includes full audit trail documentation - test execution records, coverage reports, SLA compliance logs, and quality trend analysis. This documentation is designed to satisfy enterprise procurement requirements, SOC 2 audits, and regulatory review.
